About Dua Collection

A comprehensive resource for Muslims to learn and practice duas from the Quran and Sunnah.

Our Mission

My mission is to provide an accessible, user-friendly platform for Muslims worldwide to access authentic duas for various occasions. I strive to:

  • Collect and present authentic duas from reliable Islamic sources
  • Make it easy to find duas for specific situations and needs
  • Provide accurate Arabic text, transliteration, and translation
  • Educate users about the importance and benefits of making dua
  • Create a beautiful, responsive interface that makes learning and practicing duas a pleasant experience

Sources

The duas in this collection are sourced from:

  • The Holy Quran
  • Authentic Hadith collections (Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, etc.)
  • Hisnul Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim)
  • Other authentic scholarly works

I strive to ensure the accuracy and authenticity of all content on this website. If you find any errors or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us.
