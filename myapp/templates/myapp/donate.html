Why Donate?

The Dua Collection website is maintained by volunteers dedicated to making authentic duas accessible to Muslims worldwide. Your generous donations help me to:

  • Maintain and improve the website infrastructure
  • Add new features and functionality
  • Expand our collection with more authentic duas
  • Create educational content about the importance of dua
  • Reach more Muslims around the world

The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Whoever guides someone to goodness will have a reward similar to that of its doer." (Sahih Muslim)

By contributing to this project, you're helping spread beneficial knowledge and earning ongoing rewards, inshaAllah.
